Astronomers discover new Earth-sized world orbiting an ultra-cool star

Astronomy Science

    An international team of astronomers has detected a new, Earth-sized planet just 55 light years away, orbiting an ultra-cool red dwarf star. The planet is only the second of its kind to be discovered around this type of star. Called SPECULOOS-3 b, it takes around 17 hours to complete an orbit of the star, which is more than twice as cold as our sun, as well as ten times less massive and a hundred times less luminous.
